# Internal Scientific Review Checklist

- Confirm every major claim maps to a figure, table, or cited source.
- Confirm every figure has a legend owner and source-data plan.
- Confirm every table has a canonical data source and schema.
- Confirm citation placeholders and unresolved references are gone.
- Confirm data, code, and materials availability expectations are known.
- Confirm the target venue overlay has been selected or narrowed down.
