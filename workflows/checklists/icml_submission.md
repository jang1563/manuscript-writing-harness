# ICML Submission Checklist

- Confirm the main paper body stays within the current ICML content-page limit.
- Confirm references and appendices follow in the same PDF rather than being used to hide critical claims.
- Confirm acknowledgements, grant numbers, and non-anonymized repository links are omitted from the submission build.
- Confirm any supplementary manuscripts, code, or data are anonymized before upload.
- Confirm any supporting material needed for anonymous self-citations is included in the permitted supplementary package.
