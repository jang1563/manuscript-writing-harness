# IEEE VIS Submission Checklist

- Confirm the target IEEE venue follows IEEE VIS-style area-model guidance rather than a different IEEE submission workflow.
- Confirm whether the paper is being submitted single-blind or double-blind.
- If anonymized, confirm acknowledgements, metadata, and supplementary files do not reveal authorship.
- Confirm the main paper stays within the content-page limit and extra pages contain only allowed material.
- Confirm supplemental files are optional and that core claims remain reviewable from the main paper alone.
